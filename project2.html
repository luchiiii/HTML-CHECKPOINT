SavorVine Bistro Menu

Appetizers

  • Caprese Salad

    Fresh tomatoes and mozzarella cheese slices layered with basil leaves, drizzled with balsamic glaze and extra virgin olive oil.

  • Crispy Calamari

    Tender calamari rings lightly breaded and fried to perfection, served with a zesty marinara sauce for dipping.

  • Truffle Mushroom Bruschetta

    Sautéed mushrooms infused with aromatic truffle oil, piled on toasted baguette slices and topped with Parmesan cheese and fresh herbs.

Main Courses

  • Filet Mignon

    Grilled to your preference, our tender filet mignon is served with roasted garlic mashed potatoes and seasonal vegetables, accompanied by a rich red wine reduction.

  • Pan-Seared Sea Bass

    Succulent sea bass fillet, pan-seared to perfection and served over a bed of creamy lemon risotto, accompanied by sautéed spinach and a tangy citrus beurre blanc sauce.

  • Vegetable Stuffed Portobello Mushroom

    Large portobello mushroom caps filled with a savory mixture of quinoa, roasted vegetables, and goat cheese, baked to perfection and served with a side of garlic sautéed green beans.

Desserts

  • Classic New York Cheesecake

    Creamy and smooth New York-style cheesecake on a graham cracker crust, topped with your choice of fresh berries or a decadent raspberry sauce.

  • Crème Brûlée

    Silky smooth vanilla custard with a caramelized sugar crust, served with fresh berries and a delicate tuile cookie.
